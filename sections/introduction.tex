\addsection{INTRODUCTION}

\iftoggle{printable}{}{\bigbreak}

The region surrounding the Mediterranean Sea has experienced conflict throughout most of its recorded history. During the period we know as the Ancient World, mighty empires rose and fell upon one another. Every empire’s rise and every empire’s fall were marked by war. Total War.

Based on the world-renowned video game, \pagetarget{Rome Total War}{\textbf{Rome Total War: The Board Game}} allows up to four players to play as the leaders of Rome, Carthage, Greece, and Gallo-Germania. Each player will claim territories, recruit armies, fight enemies, raise taxes, spy, riot, and more.

\textbf{Notice}: In this rule book, game and component terms are Capitalized.
\textbf{Bold text} is used to draw attention to important rules.
\textit{Italicization} is used for gameplay examples.
\pagelink{Rome Total War}{Brown colored hyperlinks} will take you to other parts of the rule book.
